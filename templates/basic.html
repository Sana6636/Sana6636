Welcome to Budgeting Basics

Mastering your finances begins with understanding the basics of budgeting. This module will guide you through the essentials of creating a budget tailored to your goals, monitoring your spending, and building savings habits.

Whether you're saving for a big goal or just trying to manage your daily expenses better, budgeting is your key to financial freedom.

Budgeting Module

Step 1: Track your expenses for the month. Write down all your purchases, bills, and subscriptions.

Step 2: Categorize your expenses into needs, wants, and savings. Assign appropriate percentages to each.

Step 3: Set a realistic budget for each category and make adjustments as needed to align with your goals.
